Research-grade landscape reviews for every capability on the cluster. Each
Paper maps the vendor space, grades options against a decision-maker rubric,
and returns to Frank's choice as a worked case study — honest about where
that choice would not generalize.

The series is twenty-one papers total: a prologue plus twenty capability
papers mapped to the layer registry. Papers are written in **decision-weight
order** (whichever vendor fight is most worth reading next) but listed below
in **architectural-stack order** (00 → 20). Status updates automatically as
new papers publish.
